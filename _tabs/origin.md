# Origin: The Truth Behind Saurabh Audit

Welcome to **Saurabh Audit**, where I (Saurabh) share everything I wish I’d known at the start—**brutal failures, real-world discoveries, and the uncensored grind of building SaaS and digital products in India.**

---

##  Why This Site Exists

There’s no shortage of founder guides or “overnight success” stories. But in most cases, **the really valuable stuff—the trauma and the tactics—gets left out**. I built Saurabh Audit for:
- _SaaS makers and indie founders who want actionable insights_
- _Bootstrapper teams who believe honesty > hype_
- _Anyone tired of “10x growth hacks” and wants to see what fails (and why)_

---

##  My Unfiltered Journey

- **Started with...**  
  My first project failed fast: zero users, zero revenue, 1000 lessons.
- **Bootstrapping & Burnout**  
  The SaaS curve isn’t up-and-to-the-right. Mistakes: not vetting users, pricing nightmares, chasing US market with an Indian team, automation gone wrong.
- **Learning (and Unlearning)**  
  Why “standard advice” often fails in local context  
  How I built/rebuilt products—tools, audits, AI projects—openly, with every dead-end written up.
- **Current Focus:**  
  - Pricing audits for Indian SaaS  
  - Founders toolkit (open, no paywall, no fluff)
  - Building in public: founder logs, teardown notes, real code/tools

---

##  What You’ll Really Find Here

- **Case Studies** – Actual pricing pages, teardown audits, and founder interviews.
- **Build In Public** – Irregular, unfiltered, “warts-and-all” logs from ongoing SaaS-building experiments.
- **Brutal AI/Automation Reviews** – Not newsletter bait, **actual limitations and founder pain-points called out—by a founder, for founders**.
- **Open Tools & Templates** – Every tool runs locally, is open, and has zero marketing fluff.
- **Resource Archive** – Downloadable checklists, teardown PDFs, and more.

If you want **optimistic, glossy SaaS narrative**—there are other sites.  
If you want _strategy, failure, and founder experiments that work in the Indian market_—you’re in the right place.
