## Where are they going?

Heatmaps tell you *where* people click. The Friction Mapper tells you *why* they stop.

### The Innovation:
Traditional analytics focus on events. We focus on **Cognitive Load**. Our engine calculates the average time taken between form fields and flags areas where users hesitate.

### The Use Case:
We deployed Friction Mapper on a Fintech SaaS and found that their 'Address Verification' step was causing a 40% bounce rate because it was too complex for mobile users.

### The Fix:
By implementing auto-complete and reducing 4 fields to 1, we recovered $12k in monthly abandoned subscriptions.

**Status:** Beta testing with 20 founders.
