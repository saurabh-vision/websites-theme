## The Math behind the Magic

Most SaaS ROI calculators are just simple multiplication. 'If you save 10 hours and pay $50/hour, you saved $500.' Saurabh Audit's ROI Engine is built for the real world.

### The Project Goals:
- Factor in **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**.
- Account for **CAC Payback Periods**.
- Handle **Tiered Pricing Models**.

### The Technical Stack:
- **Core Logic**: Pure JavaScript for instant, zero-latency feedback.
- **Data Persistence**: LocalStorage for session-based reporting.
- **Exports**: PDF generation using a headless browser on our backend.

### The Result:
A lead-generation magnet that has captured 500+ high-quality emails in the first month. Founders trust data they can actually verify.

**Status:** Live on the Tools Library.
