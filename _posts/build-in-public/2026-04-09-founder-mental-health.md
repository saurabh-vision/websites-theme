## The Dark Side of shipping

Shipping something new every day sounds heroic on LinkedIn, but in reality, it's exhausting. Building Saurabh Audit in public has been a dream, but last week, I hit a wall.

### The Symptoms:
- **Decision Paralysis**: Spending 3 hours deciding between two shades of green.
- **Refresh Addiction**: Checking Google Analytics every 10 minutes.
- **Sleep Neglect**: Thinking about Liquid tag syntax until 3 AM.

### The Intervention:
I turned off my phone, closed the laptop, and went offline for 48 hours. No code, no socials, no reviews.

### The Result:
I came back with more clarity than a month of building. I realized that 80% of what I was stressing over didn't matter to the users. They care about the audits, not the pixel-perfect border radius of a sidebar.

**Founder Tip:** You can't audit other people's products if your own mind is a mess. Rest is a feature, not a bug.
