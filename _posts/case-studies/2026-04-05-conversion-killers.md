## The Revenue Leaks

After auditing 50+ SaaS platforms in the last year, I've noticed a pattern. It's rarely the product itself that fails; it's the friction between the user and the value.

### The Top 5 Killers:

1. **The 'Sign-up for a Demo' Wall**: In 2026, if I can't try your tool without talking to a sales rep, I'm going to your competitor.
2. **Hidden Pricing**: 'Contact us' is code for 'We're going to overcharge you based on your company size.' 
3. **Broken Password Reset**: You'd be surprised how many SaaS sites have a broken 'Forgot Password' flow. That's a 100% bounce rate for returning users.
4. **No Social Proof**: If nobody else is using it, why should I? 
5. **Slow Onboarding**: If it takes more than 3 steps to reach the 'Aha!' moment, you've lost the user.

### The Fix:
We implemented 'One-Click Google Auth' and 'Interactive Sandboxes' for 3 clients, and their retention rates jumped by 15-20%.

**Final Thought:** Every extra click is a 5% drop in conversion. Count your clicks.
