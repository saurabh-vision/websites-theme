## The Feature Trap

Most founders build an incredible engine and then describe it as 'An AI-powered, cloud-native, scalable database connector.' Nobody cares. They care about their problems, not your tech stack.

### The Roast:
In this audit, we looked at a SaaS landing page with a 0.5% conversion rate. 

**The Failures:**
1. **The Hero Header**: It was vague. 'Automate your life.' Automate what? My taxes? My laundry?
2. **The Visuals**: Stock photos of people shaking hands. In a SaaS. Why?
3. **The CTA**: One button at the very bottom saying 'Submit.'

### The Refactor:
- **Benefit-First Headline**: 'Stop manually syncing your spreadsheets. Save 4 hours/week.'
- **Screenshot Proof**: Replaced stock photos with a real-time GIF of the tool in action.
- **Sticky CTA**: A 'Start Free Trial' button that stays visible as you scroll.

**Result:** Conversion jumped from 0.5% to 1.2% in 48 hours. Logic beats flair every time.
