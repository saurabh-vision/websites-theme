## You lost them at 'Hello'

In 2026, you don't have 10 seconds to explain your product. You have three. If a user can't tell exactly what your SaaS does within 3 seconds of landing, they are gone.

### The Audit:
We looked at a CRM dashboard that was trying to show 50 different metrics on the home screen. It was 'Information Overload' heaven.

### The 3-Second Test:
We showed the site to 10 random people for 3 seconds each. 
- **Result**: Only 2 could tell it was a CRM. The others thought it was a stock trading app or a generic 'dashboard builder.'

### The Refactor:
1. **The Hero**: Changed 'Your unified business intelligence ecosystem' to 'Track every sales call in one place.'
2. **Dashboard Clean-up**: Hidden 80% of the metrics behind a 'View Details' button. Only showed the 'Active Leads' and 'Revenue' at the top.
3. **Speed Optimization**: Reduced the LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) from 4.2s to 1.1s.

**Result:** Bounce rate dropped from 72% to 41%. Clarity equals cash.
