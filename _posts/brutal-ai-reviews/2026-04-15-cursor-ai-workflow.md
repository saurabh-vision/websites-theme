## Stop Typing, Start Composing

Cursor isn't just VS Code with a ChatGPT plugin. It's a fundamental rethink of the IDE. If you're a solo founder, your goal isn't to be a great coder; it's to ship great products.

### The Power Trio:
1. **Context Indexing (@Repo)**: Cursor knows your whole project. It knows that the `_tabs/tools.md` file depends on `_data/tools.yml`. It won't suggest hallucinatory paths.
2. **Composer (Cmd+I)**: This is the highlight. You describe a feature—like 'Add a filter bar to my tools page'—and it generates the HTML, CSS, and JS across multiple files simultaneously.
3. **Chat-to-Diff**: Gone are the days of copy-pasting code blocks. One hit of 'Apply' and your code is merged.

### The Warning:
Don't let the AI drive the architecture. If you don't define your CSS variables (like `--accent-color`) beforehand, the AI will invent inline styles that will haunt you during maintenance.

**Pro Tip:** Always index your project before a big refactor. It takes 30 seconds but saves 30 minutes of debugging.
