## The Verdict: 4.5/5 Rockets 🚀

If you're still using GPT-4o for complex logic, you're living in the past. Claude 3.5 Sonnet isn't just a minor upgrade; it's a fundamental shift in how AI handles large codebase context.

### Why it Wins for Founders:
1. **Architectural Awareness**: It doesn't just write functions; it understands how your CSS, HTML, and Liquid tags interact.
2. **Reduced Hallucinations**: When it doesn't know, it (usually) says so. GPT-4o often tries to 'fake it till it makes it,' which leads to broken production builds.
3. **Artifacts Feature**: Being able to see a preview of what you're building in real-time is a game-changer for rapid prototyping.

### The Friction Points:
- The rate limits are still aggressive. 
- It occasionally misses subtle Ruby/Liquid syntax nuances that Jekyll requires.

**Bottom line:** If you're building a SaaS in 2026, Sonnet is your lead dev. GPT is your junior.
